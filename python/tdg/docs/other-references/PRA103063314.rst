
Pilati, Orso, Bertaina. Phys. Rev. A, 103:063314, (2021)

Ref. :cite:`Pilati:2021` performs simulations of spin-imbalanced systems.  They define :math:`P = (N_{\uparrow} - N_{\downarrow}) / N` and control :math:`P` by varying the chemical potentials of the different spins independently.
